About FROGG Recruitment

Top Recruitment Agency

Since 2010

Frogg Recruitment, a top recruitment agency established in 2010, plays a significant role in today’s employment and career enhancement landscape. The company offers valuable benefits, including access to a wider talent pool, expertise in candidate evaluation, and improved efficiency in the hiring process.

Read more about our services

FROGG Recruitment is widely regarded as one of the most respected recruitment agencies in South Africa. With our head office in Cape Town and a strong presence in Johannesburg and Pretoria, we provide reliable and professional recruitment solutions to businesses nationwide.

Clients benefit from our reliable and professional recruitment services, helping them secure both permanent and contract staff across South Africa. We work with a wide range of industries and sectors, delivering tailored employment solutions backed by over 50 years of combined recruitment experience.

Are you looking for a reliable employment agency to help you find staff? Our recruitment services are finely tuned to perfection, ensuring we help our clients secure the ideal candidates for both permanent and contract positions.
