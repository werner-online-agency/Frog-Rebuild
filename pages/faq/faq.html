Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our recruitment services, process, and how we can help you.

For Candidates

You can register by visiting our website and clicking "Sign In / Up" to create a candidate profile. Alternatively, you can email your CV directly to info@froggsa.co.za and one of our consultants will be in touch.

No, our services are completely free for candidates. Recruitment fees are paid by the employer, so there is never a cost to you for being placed through Frogg Recruitment.

Timelines vary depending on the role and client requirements. Typically, the process takes 2–6 weeks from initial application to placement. Your consultant will keep you updated throughout.

We recruit across a wide range of industries and levels — from junior through to executive positions. Our specialities include finance, IT, engineering, sales, marketing, manufacturing, healthcare, and more. Visit our services page for the full list.

Absolutely. We treat all candidate information with the strictest confidence. Your CV will never be sent to a client without your prior consent, and all personal data is managed in compliance with the POPI Act.

For Employers

Our fees are competitive and typically based on a percentage of the successful candidate's annual salary for permanent placements. For temporary and contract roles, we charge an hourly or daily rate. Contact us for a tailored quote.

We offer a guarantee period on all permanent placements. If the candidate leaves or is let go within the guarantee period, we will find a replacement at no additional cost. Specific terms are outlined in our service agreement.

We use a thorough multi-step process that includes CV screening, competency-based interviews, reference checks, criminal record checks, and psychometric assessments where applicable. We only present candidates we are confident will be the right fit.

Yes, we provide full temporary and contract staffing solutions including payroll management, labour compliance, and ongoing support. Whether you need one person or an entire team, we can scale to your needs.

General

Frogg Recruitment has offices in Cape Town and Johannesburg, allowing us to service clients and candidates across South Africa. Visit our contact page for full address details.

You can reach us by phone at +27 21 556 5598 (Cape Town) or +27 10 824 6772 (Johannesburg), or email info@froggsa.co.za. You can also use our online contact form.
