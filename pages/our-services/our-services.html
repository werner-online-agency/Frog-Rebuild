Our Expertise

How We Can Help

Tailored recruitment solutions designed to meet your unique business needs

01

Permanent Recruitment

Full-service permanent placement from job profiling and candidate sourcing through to onboarding support. We ensure the right cultural and skills fit for lasting appointments.

02

Temporary & Contract Staffing

Flexible workforce solutions for project-based work, seasonal peaks, and fixed-term requirements. Fully managed payroll and compliance included.

03

Executive Search

Confidential headhunting for C-suite, director, and senior management positions. We leverage our extensive industry networks to identify exceptional leaders.

04

Specialist Recruitment

Niche recruitment for hard-to-fill roles requiring specialist skills or qualifications. Our consultants have deep sector expertise across multiple industries.

05

Payroll Services

Comprehensive payroll administration and management for your temporary and contract staff, ensuring compliance with all South African labour legislation.

06

Psychometric Assessments

Industry-standard assessments and skills testing to validate candidate suitability and ensure the best possible match for your organisation.
