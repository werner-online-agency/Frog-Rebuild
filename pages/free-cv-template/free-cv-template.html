CV Writing Tips

Follow these tips from our recruitment experts to create a CV that gets noticed

01

Keep It Concise

Aim for 2–3 pages maximum. Recruiters scan CVs quickly — make every word count and focus on your most relevant experience.

02

Tailor for Each Role

Customise your CV for each application. Highlight skills and achievements that match the specific job requirements.

03

Quantify Achievements

Use numbers and percentages to demonstrate impact. "Increased sales by 30%" is far more compelling than "improved sales".

04

Professional Format

Use a clean, consistent layout with clear headings. Avoid excessive graphics that can confuse applicant tracking systems.

05

Include Keywords

Mirror the language used in the job description. Many companies use automated screening before a human sees your CV.

06

Proofread Carefully

Spelling and grammar errors can cost you the interview. Have someone else review your CV before submitting.
